Greenwich Fitness

"After LA Fitness, this gym is a breath of fresh air. An inspiring balance between working out upstairs in the gym to spending an equal amount of time in the mixed sauna, steam room working towards a final pampering in the changing rooms, where they even have magnifying mirrors for your face. Car parking is not a problem, there is also a bar. I wish I owned it." Mark Twain


"Very good for the price. No frills, sauna or pool. But fine if all you want is a good workout. In need of a few back hyper extension platforms. These old school platforms are much better and safer than the naff back machines they have. These are great bits of kit, sadly forgotten in most modern gyms... I used to be a member of Virgin, and they didn't have them either." Graham S.


"In my honest opinion this health club has all the technical arsenal you might need for your fitness training-the amount of high-tech machinery this gym will gaurantee that your fitness regime will go off with an almighty bang. The centre is actually located in a delightful listed building:its a converted armoury with a really high ceiling and lots of natural light. Keep in mind that it is a charity based gym and all the profits are ploughed back into providing more leisure facilities for the local community."Tatiana Snow
